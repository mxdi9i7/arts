2016 CONFERENCE HIGHLIGHTS

Bring back memories

Mr. Lezhi Wang，painter of tradition- al Chinese landscape of mountain and rivers:

He spends almost all his time in Bei- jing, the capital for 800 years the home of Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, Summer Palace, embraced by Yan Mountain in the northwest and guarded by West Mountain and surrounded by Yongding River, Chao- bai River and Beijing Hangzhou Grand Canal. ;

In the past 20 years, he became a full time painter spending his time on touring more than 30 provinces, visiting the magnificent mountains and rivers including Huangshan, Qiling Moutain, Shengnongjia, Zhangjiajie, which laid foundation for his character. Furthermore, he went abroad to ex- pand his vision including Siamese Buddhist temple, Dongying Gar- den, Malacca Strait, Colorado Grand Canyon, Yosemite Redwood Forest, Niagara Falls....;

He went through 6 decades of life with all kinds of difficulties and devot- ed 50 years of time in painting. Now he is presenting his dozens of works painted in recent 10 years with is heart and might.



Bird-and-flower painting

Bird-and-flower painting is a kind of Chinese painting named after its subject matter. Normally, most bird-and-flower paintings belong to the scholar-artist style of Chinese painting.



Shan shui

Shan shui refers to a style of traditional Chinese painting that involves or depicts scenery or natural landscapes, using a brush and ink rather than more conventional paints. Mountains, rivers and often waterfalls are prominent in this art form.
